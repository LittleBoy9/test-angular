WhatsApp is a popular messaging app that allows users to send and receive text messages, voice messages, photos, videos, documents, and other types of files. It was founded in 2009 and later acquired by Facebook in 2014. WhatsApp is available on both Android and iOS devices and has become a widely used communication tool around the world. The app uses end-to-end encryption to protect users' privacy and security, and it also offers features like group chats, voice and video calls, and status updates. With its user-friendly interface and free messaging capabilities, WhatsApp has become an essential tool for personal and professional communication.
